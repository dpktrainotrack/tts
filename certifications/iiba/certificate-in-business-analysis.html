IIBA Entry Certificate in Business Analysis (ECBA) certification is a foundational-level credential designed to validate candidate’s knowledge of basic business analysis concepts, techniques, and terminology. It is suitable for individuals from both business and IT backgrounds who want to begin their journey into the business analysis profession. The certification aims to establish a solid understanding of BA fundamentals, requirement gathering, stakeholder communication, and process improvement, making it an ideal entry point for anyone aspiring to enter the business analysis domain.

The ECBA exam familiarizes candidates with essential BA knowledge areas from the BABOK® Guide, including requirements lifecycle management, business analysis planning, elicitation, and requirement modeling techniques. Alongside that, it covers business analysis roles, key concepts, stakeholder perspectives, and solution evaluation basics. The candidate also develops awareness of IIBA’s global BA standards, professional development framework, and the value of structured analysis practices in projects.

IIBA ECBA certification is the perfect starting point for students, fresh graduates, and early-career professionals who want to explore business analysis as a career path. It builds the confidence to pursue advanced IIBA certifications like CCBA and CBAP, which are more experience-driven.
