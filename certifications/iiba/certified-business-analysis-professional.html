Certified Business Analysis Professional (CBAP) Certification is an advanced-level credential designed to validate candidate’s expertise in business analysis, requirements management, and solution evaluation. It is ideally suited for professionals with significant experience in business analysis who want to demonstrate mastery of advanced concepts, practices, and frameworks aligned with the BABOK® Guide. The certification establishes credibility as a senior-level business analyst capable of leading complex projects and delivering value to business stakeholders.

Alongside that, it emphasizes decision-making, leadership skills, and the ability to apply business analysis techniques in real-world scenarios. Prerequisites

  • Mandatory Requirements:
    • Minimum 7,500 hours of business analysis work experience in the last 10 years.
    • At least 900 hours have been completed in four of the six knowledge areas of BABOK® Guide.
    • Minimum of 35 hours of professional development in the last 4 years.
    • Two references from a career manager, client, or CBAP recipient.
  • Recommended Knowledge:
    • Strong understanding of business analysis frameworks and practices.
    • Hands-on experience with requirement gathering, stakeholder communication, and solution design.

Ideal For: Experienced business analysts, project leads, consultants, product managers, and professionals aiming to demonstrate senior-level business analysis expertise.
