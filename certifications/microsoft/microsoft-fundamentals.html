The Microsoft Fundamentals (900 Series) certifications are entry-level credentials designed to validate foundational knowledge across Microsoft technologies. These certifications are intended for candidates with both technical and non-technical backgrounds, aiming to demonstrate a basic understanding of cloud concepts, Microsoft services, pricing, security, compliance, and support.

The Microsoft 900 series exams serve as the first step toward advanced Microsoft certifications. They introduce learners to Microsoft’s cloud ecosystem, including Azure, Microsoft 365, Dynamics 365, Power Platform, and security concepts. Candidates also gain familiarity with Microsoft’s responsibility model, service architecture, subscription management, and common use cases.

Microsoft Fundamental Certifications are best suited for individuals who want to begin their Microsoft journey without needing deep technical expertise. It provides a strong base of knowledge and prepares learners to progress toward associate and expert-level Microsoft certifications.

Prerequisites

  • No Mandatory Requirements: Open to anyone regardless of prior technical knowledge.
  • Recommended Knowledge:
    • Basic IT awareness (servers, networking, storage).
    • Understanding of business applications and cloud usage.
  • Ideal For: Students, entry-level professionals, business managers, IT newcomers, and anyone beginning with Microsoft technologies.
