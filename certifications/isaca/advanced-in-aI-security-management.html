Advanced in AI Security Management (AAISM) certification is a top-notch professional-level certification. It is aimed primarily at the technical staff as well as the management from different organizations that possess and use AI systems and are in charge of the corresponding security measures. Holding this certificate, they demonstrate their competence in the implementation of AI security methods and the achievement of related types of organizational objectives.

AAISM certification ensures that candidates must know about AI risk frameworks, regulatory compliance, ethical AI governance, data privacy, and security considerations in AI-driven environments. These topics are also linked to AI lifecycle management, governance models, and risk assessment strategies.

The AAISM credential is the best way to become familiar with the concept of AI security and governance among professionals without requiring the development of skills in coding and software development. It gives a good start for further progression to the ISACA higher-level credentials in governance, risk, and compliance.

Prerequisites

  • No Mandatory Requirements: Open to professionals from technical and non-technical domains.
  • Recommended Knowledge:
    • Basic awareness of AI systems and data workflows.
    • General understanding of IT risk, compliance, and cybersecurity.
  • Ideal For: Risk managers, compliance officers, IT security professionals, AI product managers, governance professionals, and business leaders responsible for AI adoption.

Benefits of ISACA AAISM Certification

  • Validates AI Security Expertise – Demonstrates your ability to manage and secure AI systems.
  • Career Advancement – Opens opportunities in AI governance, risk management, and compliance.
  • Bridges Tech & Business – Positions you as a link between AI teams and business leadership.
  • Pathway to Higher ISACA Certifications – Builds a foundation for certifications like CRISC, CISM, or CGEIT.
