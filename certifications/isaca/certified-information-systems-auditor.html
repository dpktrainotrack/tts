The ISACA Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA) certification is a top-tier credential that is acknowledged worldwide for professional staff who carry out audits, oversee, and evaluate information systems. It is a certification that reflects the mastery of the speaker in information systems auditing, control, security, and governance.

This certification confirms the knowledge of the person in the areas of vulnerability evaluation, compliance report, and the setting up of organizational controls. It is considered the most valued by employers all over the world, who use it as a measure of the capability of the IT auditors and security professionals.

The CISA certification exam aims to test the candidate's skills in IS audit standards application, guidelines, and best practices, along with the knowledge of IT governance, risk management, and assurance processes. CISA Certification-holder candidates present their competencies in system security and regulatory compliance; as a result, it is a must-have credential for personnel in an Auditor and IT Governance Career.

Prerequisites

No Mandatory Requirements: Anyone can attempt the exam.

  • Mandatory Requirements: A minimum of five years of professional experience in information systems auditing, control, or security.
  • Experience Waivers: Up to three years can be waived for:
    • One year of IS or non-IS auditing experience
    • One year of information systems or security experience
    • Two years of university teaching in a related field
    • 120 university credit hours substitute for one year
    • A master’s degree in IS/IT from a recognized institution substitutes for one year
  • Recommended Knowledge :
    • Basic to advanced IT controls and security concepts
    • Understanding of IT governance and compliance frameworks
    • Knowledge of business processes and risk management
  • Ideal For: IT auditors, compliance officers, security professionals, risk management specialists, and anyone looking to advance in IT governance or auditing careers.

Benefits of ISACA CISA Certification

  • Global recognition - One of the most respected certifications in IT audit and control.
  • Career advancement – Qualifies you for senior-level audit, risk, and compliance roles.
