CompTIA SecurityX (Formerly CASP+) is a cybersecurity certification of the highest level, which is targeted at security professionals with prior experience, aiming to prove their technical skills in enterprise security. Differing from manager-level certifications, the focus of the SecurityX certification exam is on practical skills for risk assessment, the expansion of the enterprise security solution, and the implementation of incident response and architecture solutions.

The SecurityX credential is intended for security staff rather than a management team; hence, it is unlike any other advanced security certification. It confirms expertise in enterprise security architecture, governance, risk management, encryption, and the technologies that arise, like cloud, mobile, and IoT security. The SecurityX exam is to certify that professionals can use a critical thinking approach and make the right call across various security fields.

The CompTIA SecurityX certification is considered the best option for those who want to stay highly technical in cybersecurity and, at the same time, show their capacity for dealing with complicated enterprise-level issues. This certification is internationally known and is the first choice for both government and business organizations as a measure of the highest security expertise.
