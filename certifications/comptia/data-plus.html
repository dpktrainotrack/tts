CompTIA Data+ carries a lower-level certification suitable for those new to data, which outlines the basic data concepts, data environments, data governance, and data visualization topics. The focus is on the four core activities of data mining, data manipulation, data reporting, and data compliance. Moreover, it unveils the widespread practice of data analysis in business and IT, thus being adaptable to any industry-oriented background.

The CompTIA Data+ Certification can be the best choice for those who want to become data analysts without worrying about their technical skills and programming knowledge. It acts like a stepping stone to the higher-level certification or specialized data roles.

Prerequisites

  • Requirements: 18-24 months of experience required in a business analyst or reporting role.
    • Valid AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner or Associate-level certification (recommended but not required).
    • 5+ years of hands-on experience in designing and implementing network solutions.
    • Advanced knowledge of networking concepts such as TCP/IP, DNS, VPNs, firewalls, and hybrid connectivity.
  • Recommended Knowledge:
    • Basic understanding of databases and business operations.
    • General IT awareness (spreadsheets and data visualization).
  • Ideal For: Students, entry-level professionals, business managers, marketing teams, and IT newcomers starting their data journey.
