Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP) certification is recognized all over the world that belongs to the Project Management Institute (PMI) and is used to indicate the knowledge of six core agile principles, common practices, tools, and techniques that are used in different agile approaches. The certification changes the swearing of the agile spectrum by unifying Scrum, Kanban, Lean, extreme programming (XP), and test-driven development (TDD).

A PMI-ACP certification is for the members of the agile team and the professionals whose companies are undergoing an agile transformation. It consists of the implementation of the agile theoretical framework in practical projects, managing iterative development, stakeholder engagement, and value delivery.

ACP certification is a nice proof of qualification for project managers, team leaders, developers, or any person who wants to widen their agile knowledge and get their agility skills confirmed at a professional level.

Prerequisites

  • Four-year degree with 36 months of project management experience
  • OR secondary degree (high school diploma, associate degree) with 60 months of project management experience
  • 35 hours of project management education/training OR CAPM certification
  • Mandatory Requirements:
    • Secondary degree (high school diploma, associate’s degree, or global equivalent).
    • 21 contact hours of training in agile practices.
    • 12 months of general project experience in the last 5 years (a current PMP® or PgMP® satisfies this requirement).
    • 8 months of agile project experience in the last 3 years.
  • Recommended Knowledge:
    • Understanding of agile frameworks (Scrum, Kanban, Lean).
    • Experience working in or with cross-functional teams.
  • Ideal For:

    Project managers, agile practitioners, scrum masters, team leads, product owners, and professionals seeking recognition in agile project management.

Benefits of PMI-ACP Certification

  • Validates Agile Skills – Demonstrates practical agile knowledge and competency across multiple frameworks.
  • Career Advancement – Opens up opportunities for agile roles with higher responsibilities.
  • Global Recognition – PMI is trusted worldwide, making this certification highly respected.
  • Adaptability – Equips you to apply agile techniques across diverse industries and projects.
