Juniper Associate Certifications are foundational-level credentials designed to validate candidates’ understanding of Juniper Networks technologies, products, and networking principles. These certifications are ideal for individuals from both technical and non-technical backgrounds who want to gain a strong grounding in networking, security, automation, and AI-driven solutions. They provide an entry point into Juniper’s certification track, making them suitable for beginners and IT professionals seeking to enhance their knowledge.

The Juniper Associate-level exams introduce candidates to key Juniper solutions, covering routing and switching, network design, Mist AI-driven networking, and security practices. Candidates also develop awareness of Juniper’s role in enterprise networking, its product ecosystem, and centralized management approaches. Juniper associate certifications act as a stepping stone for professionals aspiring to build a career in networking, security, and cloud-based infrastructures.

The Associate certifications include:

  • Design, Associate (JNCIA-Design) – JN0-105
  • Junos, Associate (JNCIA-Junos) – JN0-351 & JN0-363
  • Mist AI, Associate (JNCIA-MistAI) – JN0-452
  • Security, Associate (JNCIA-SEC) – JN0-335

Each certification targets specific skill areas while maintaining a common foundation, helping learners align with industry needs and Juniper’s technology roadmap.
