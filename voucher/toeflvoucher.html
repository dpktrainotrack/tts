About the Result Process

The results for TOEFL iBT are typically accessible online within 4–8 days after the date of the test. Once scores become available, ETS (Educational Testing Service) sends an email notification with step-by-step guidance on how to get access to them. Candidates can go to their ETS account and have a look at their official score report and even print out a PDF copy for safekeeping.

Through the same account, students can also request their scores to be sent directly to universities, colleges, or professional institutions. Testers are given four score reports at no cost if the institutions are selected before the examination, and more reports can be sent upon payment of a fee.

TOEFL iBT is a test that is evaluated on a scale of 0–120, and every one of the four parts (Reading, Listening, Speaking, and Writing) is given a score out of 30. The division of the score makes it easier for candidates to recognize the parts in which they are strong and also their weak points.

Key Highlights of the TOEFL Result Process:

  • Within 4–8 days after taking the test, the results can be accessed.
  • A message is sent to the user's email inbox, notifying them that the score is available.
  • Through the ETS account, the test results can be viewed and also saved in PDF format.
  • The option of score delivery to schools is available.
  • The score range is from 0 to 120, with every section contributing 30 points.
  • Justification of scoring is the use of both AI and human judgment, with fairness and reliability being the result.

How to Prepare for Exam Day

  • Arrive Early: Reach the TOEFL test center at least 30 minutes before the reporting time to avoid last-minute stress or delays.
  • Have a Proper Meal: Do not skip breakfast. Eat a healthy meal to stay energized, and consider carrying a light snack or fruit for sustained energy.
  • Dress Comfortably: Wear loose and comfortable attire suitable for all temperatures, ensuring you remain relaxed throughout the TOEFL exam.
  • Carry Valid Identification: Bring your passport as proof of identification. At the test centre, your photograph and biometrics will be captured and verified for official purposes.
