    About the Result Process

    The PTE Academic results are usually available online within 2–5 business days after the exam. Candidates first receive an email notification within five business days with detailed instructions on how to access their results. Using this email, students can log in to their Pearson account through the PTE Academic student portal to view their scores. From the same account, results can also be shared directly with universities, colleges, or other institutions.

    The PTE Academic exam is scored on a scale of 10 to 90, with results displayed in a graphical format. This makes it easier for candidates to understand their strengths and identify areas for improvement. The scoring reflects a candidate’s ability to read, write, listen, and speak effectively in English.

    To enhance security, PTE now combines AI-based scoring with human verification. This dual-check system ensures results are accurate, fair, and helps detect any copied or fraudulent content.
